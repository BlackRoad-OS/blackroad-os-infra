## ğŸ“– Checklist Categories Explained

### ğŸ· CLASSIFY
Determine the communication type based on audience size and composition:
- **Retail Communication**: Distributed to more than 25 retail investors within 30 days
- **Correspondence**: Sent to 25 or fewer retail investors within 30 days
- **Institutional Communication**: Distributed only to institutional investors

### â­• BD GATES (Broker-Dealer)
Required approvals and reviews for broker-dealer communications:
- Retail communications require principal approval before use or filing
- New broker-dealers must file retail communications 10 days before use (first year)
- Correspondence requires post-distribution review if not pre-reviewed
- Institutional communications require training, documentation, and surveillance systems

### ğŸ“£ IA AD GATES (Investment Adviser)
Investment adviser advertising restrictions:
- No testimonials allowed (social media "likes" may count as testimonials)
- Cannot imply SEC/Administrator endorsement
- Charts and formulas must disclose limitations and difficulties

### ğŸ“ EVIDENCE PACK
Documentation requirements for investment advisers:
- Keep copies of all advertisements/notices sent to 10+ persons
- Document reasons for recommending specific securities

### ğŸ—‚ RETAIN TAGS
Record retention periods:
- 6 years: Customer account records after closing
- 3 years: Communications with the public
- 4 years: Written customer complaints
- 2 years: Most recent records must be readily accessible

### ğŸ” STORAGE RULES
Electronic recordkeeping requirements:
- Records must be true, legible, and accurate
- No alteration or destruction allowed
- Electronic storage must be non-rewriteable and non-erasable
- Notify FINRA at least 90 days before implementing electronic storage

### ğŸ—ƒ IA RETENTION CLOCK
Investment adviser specific retention:
- Books and records: 5 years, readily accessible
