## Glossary

### Key Terms

**BD (Broker-Dealer)**: Firm registered to buy and sell securities on behalf of customers or for its own account

**IA (Investment Advisor)**: Person or firm that provides investment advice for compensation

**IAR (Investment Advisor Representative)**: Individual who provides advisory services on behalf of an IA

**RIA (Registered Investment Advisor)**: Investment advisor registered with SEC or state regulators

**SRO (Self-Regulatory Organization)**: Non-governmental organization with authority to create and enforce industry regulations (e.g., FINRA)

**CRD (Central Registration Depository)**: FINRA's database of registration and licensing information

**WSPs (Written Supervisory Procedures)**: Firm's documented supervisory framework

**CCO (Chief Compliance Officer)**: Senior officer responsible for firm's compliance program

**Form U4**: Uniform Application for Securities Industry Registration or Transfer

**Form BD**: Uniform Application for Broker-Dealer Registration

**BrokerCheck**: Public website to research background of brokers and firms
