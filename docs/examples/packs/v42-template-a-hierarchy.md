## Key Points

- **FINRA operates under SEC authority** as a Self-Regulatory Organization
- **Each layer regulates the layer below** it
- **Clients are protected** by the entire regulatory framework (7 layers)
- **Member firms** must register with FINRA
- **Principals** supervise representatives and maintain compliance
- **Representatives** are the front-line professionals serving clients
