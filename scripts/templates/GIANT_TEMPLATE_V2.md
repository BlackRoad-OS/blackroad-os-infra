## Tips for Effective Use

1. **Start Fresh**: Copy the template for each new major project or epic
2. **Update Regularly**: Refresh progress indicators during standups or weekly reviews
3. **Be Visual**: The emoji system provides at-a-glance status without reading details
4. **Add Context**: Feel free to add notes, links, or emoji flags for special situations
5. **Adapt to Your Needs**: Modify sections or add custom rows for your team's workflow
6. **Track Multiple Pods**: Use the pod version when coordinating across teams
7. **Archive When Done**: Keep completed templates for historical reference and retrospectives
