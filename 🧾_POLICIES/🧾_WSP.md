## Purpose

This Workplace Safety Policy establishes standards and procedures to ensure a safe working environment for all BlackRoad OS team members, contractors, and visitors.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees and contractors
- Physical and digital workspaces
- Remote work environments
- Third-party facilities used for BlackRoad OS activities

## Key Principles

### Physical Safety
- Maintain safe physical work environments
- Ensure proper ergonomic setup for all workstations
- Provide necessary safety equipment and training
- Conduct regular safety assessments

### Digital Safety
- Secure access to systems and data
- Protection against cyber threats
- Safe handling of sensitive information
- Incident reporting and response procedures

### Mental Health & Well-being
- Promote work-life balance
- Provide mental health resources
- Foster inclusive and respectful culture
- Zero tolerance for harassment

## Incident Reporting

- Direct supervisor notification

- Role-specific safety training
- Incident response procedures

- Annually by leadership team
- After any significant incident
- When regulatory requirements change
