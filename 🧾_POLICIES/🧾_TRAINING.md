## Purpose

This Training Policy defines the requirements, procedures, and standards for employee training and development within BlackRoad OS infrastructure operations.

## Scope

This policy covers:
- Onboarding training for new team members
- Ongoing technical skill development
- Compliance and regulatory training
- Security awareness training
- Role-specific certifications

## Training Requirements

### New Employee Onboarding

All new team members must complete within first 30 days:

1. **Infrastructure Orientation** (Week 1)
   - Repository structure and conventions
   - Access provisioning and security
   - Communication channels
   - Emergency procedures

2. **Technical Foundations** (Week 2-3)
   - Infrastructure-as-Code (Terraform)
   - CI/CD pipelines
   - Deployment procedures
   - Monitoring and observability

3. **Compliance & Security** (Week 4)
   - Security policies and procedures
   - Data handling requirements
   - Incident response protocols
   - Regulatory requirements

### Ongoing Training

**Required Annual Training**:
- Security awareness (annually)
- Compliance updates (annually)
- Emergency response procedures (annually)
- New policy reviews (as needed)

**Role-Specific Training**:
- Platform engineering: Cloud provider certifications
- SRE: Incident management and chaos engineering
- Security: Advanced security certifications
- Compliance: Regulatory framework training

## Training Delivery Methods

- Self-paced online courses
- Instructor-led workshops
- Hands-on labs and simulations
- Peer mentoring and shadowing
- External certifications and conferences

## Training Records

All training must be documented in `ðŸ—‚_RECORDS/`:
- Completion certificates
- Training attendance records
- Assessment results
- Certification renewals

**Retention Period**: 7 years after employment ends

## Training Assessment

Training effectiveness is measured through:
- Completion rates and timelines
- Assessment scores
- Practical application reviews
- Incident reduction metrics
- Team feedback surveys

## Responsibilities

### Employees
- Complete required training on schedule
- Maintain certifications as required
- Apply learned skills in daily work
- Provide feedback on training effectiveness

### Managers
- Ensure team members complete required training
- Identify skill gaps and training needs
- Support ongoing professional development
- Track and report training compliance

### Training Coordinators
- Develop and maintain training materials
- Schedule and facilitate training sessions
- Track completion and maintain records
- Update content based on feedback and changes

## Budget and Resources

Training budget allocation:
- 5% of personnel budget for training
- Conference attendance (2 per person/year)
- Certification exam fees covered
- Training materials and subscriptions

## Review and Updates

This policy is reviewed:
- Annually by leadership
- After significant organizational changes
- When new compliance requirements emerge
- Based on training effectiveness metrics
