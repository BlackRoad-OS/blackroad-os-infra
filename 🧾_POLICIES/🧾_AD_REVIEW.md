## Purpose

This policy establishes procedures for reviewing, approving, and documenting all advertising and promotional communications to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and brand standards.

## Scope

This policy applies to all advertising and promotional materials including:
- Digital advertisements (social media, display ads, search ads)
- Email marketing campaigns
- SMS/text message campaigns
- Website promotional content
- Public announcements and press releases
- Sponsored content and partnerships

## Review Requirements

### Pre-Publication Review

All advertising materials must be reviewed and approved **before** publication:

1. **Content Review** - Marketing team validates messaging
2. **Legal Review** - Legal team ensures regulatory compliance
3. **Brand Review** - Brand team confirms alignment with guidelines
4. **Technical Review** - Engineering team validates claims and capabilities
5. **Executive Approval** - Leadership sign-off for major campaigns

### Review Timeline

- Standard materials: 3-5 business days
- High-priority campaigns: 1-2 business days (with advance notice)
- Minor updates to approved templates: 1 business day
- Emergency communications: Same day (executive override required)

## Documentation Requirements

All advertising materials must maintain:

### Pre-Launch Documentation
- Creative briefs and campaign objectives
- Draft copy and visual assets
- Target audience and placement details
- Budget and timeline

### Review Process Documentation
- Review comments from each stakeholder
- Revision history
- Approval signatures and dates
- Legal compliance checklists

### Post-Launch Documentation
- Final published versions (screenshots/exports)
- Performance metrics and results
- Audience reach and engagement
- Any complaints or issues raised

## Compliance Checklist

All advertisements must comply with:

- [ ] Truth in advertising standards
- [ ] Data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
- [ ] Industry-specific regulations
- [ ] Platform-specific advertising policies
- [ ] Accessibility requirements (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- [ ] Brand guidelines and voice
- [ ] Legal disclaimers (where required)
- [ ] Intellectual property rights

## Prohibited Content

The following are strictly prohibited in all advertising:
- False or misleading claims
- Unsubstantiated performance claims
- Unauthorized use of trademarks or copyrighted material
- Discriminatory or offensive content
- Privacy violations or unauthorized use of personal data
- Comparative claims without substantiation

## Post-Publication Monitoring

After publication, campaigns must be monitored for:
- Compliance with published version
- Public feedback and complaints
- Performance against objectives
